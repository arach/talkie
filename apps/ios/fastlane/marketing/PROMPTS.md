# Generated campaign-background prompts

Mode: built-in image generation. The resulting images are used only as
background material; the Talkie UI, icon, and typography are composed from
project sources by `compose.swift`.

```text

Asset type: App Store screenshot background, portrait
Primary request: Create a premium abstract background for Talkie, a voice capture and writing app that turns speech into action.

Style/medium: refined editorial product-design illustration, analog tape-machine warmth meets precise modern Apple-like minimalism, photoreal material detail but abstract composition
Composition/framing: portrait 9:19.5, generous quiet negative space in the upper third for a headline, clean central area for an overlaid phone UI, visual interest concentrated near edges and bottom, no border
Lighting/mood: warm studio light, quietly magical, intelligent, trustworthy
Color palette: warm cream #F4ECD8, charcoal #16130F, amber #C58A2A, restrained soft coral accent #D96B5F
Constraints: background only; no phone, no device, no app UI, no logo, no letters, no words, no numerals, no watermark; crisp premium finish; keep important decoration away from center
Avoid: neon gradients, generic purple AI imagery, brain icons, microphones, stock-photo look, busy noise
```
