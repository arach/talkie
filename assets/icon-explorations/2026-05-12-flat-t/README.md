# flat-t

Solid landmark T in cream on canvas, Hot Mic dot as accent. No gradients, no effects. Maximum restraint.

**What it evokes.** Brutalist confidence. A signpost, not a decoration. The forward-leaning crossbar and descender hook carry the brand without any embellishment. The lone orange dot reads as a status indicator — "recording."

**Where it shines / where it fails.** Reads clearly at every size from 1024 down to 16px — the high contrast and simple geometry survive aggressive downscaling. Works equally well on iOS rounded mask and macOS squircle. The simplicity is its strength and weakness: it won't turn heads on the App Store, but it will never look dated.
