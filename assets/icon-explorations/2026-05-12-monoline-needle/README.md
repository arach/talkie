# monoline-needle

Landmark T as a single uniform-weight stroke — no fill. The "instrument needle" metaphor taken literally. Hollow Hot Mic dot ring in orange. Tiny filled dot at the hook terminus as a precision accent.

**What it evokes.** A measurement tool, a gauge needle, a draughtsman's line. Pure geometry. The hollow dot and terminal accent suggest precision without complexity. It's the most typographically honest direction — closest to how the T lives in the wordmark as a structural form.

**Where it shines / where it fails.** Elegant and distinctive at 512+. The uniform stroke weight is a strong graphic identity statement. **Fails below 40px** — the 8px stroke collapses to sub-pixel at small sizes and the mark becomes invisible. The weakest small-size performer alongside glass-vision. This direction works only if paired with a filled small-size variant for Watch/favicon.
