# glass-vision

Frosted translucent T over a warm dark surface, with inner shadow and a subtle highlight along the crossbar top edge. Soft halo around the Hot Mic dot.

**What it evokes.** Apple Vision Pro aesthetic — spatial, layered, material. The T feels like it's hovering on a frosted pane. Modern, premium, quiet. The dot's halo suggests an ambient awareness indicator.

**Where it shines / where it fails.** Striking at 1024 — the frosted materiality is convincing and contemporary. On macOS squircle with alpha, the translucency interacts beautifully with the Dock. **Struggles significantly below 64px** — the low contrast between the glass T and the background causes the mark to disappear. Would need a small-size variant with higher opacity for Watch and favicon use. The weakest performer at small sizes in this set.
