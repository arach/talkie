# neon-onair

Landmark T rendered as a thin stroke with soft luminous orange glow on near-black canvas. The Hot Mic dot blazes — bright core fading through concentric halos. Recording studio "ON AIR" lamp aesthetic.

**What it evokes.** The red/amber recording light in a studio control room. Quiet intensity. The T is the instrument, the dot is the signal. Together they say: "capturing." The double-stroke neon effect (orange outer, warm inner core) gives depth without weight.

**Where it shines / where it fails.** Stunning at 1024 and makes a gorgeous OG social card. The glow effect is genuinely atmospheric. At 40px it's still recognizable thanks to the bright dot anchor. **Below 32px the stroke T becomes a single-pixel line** — the glow can't survive downscaling. Would need a thicker-stroke small-size variant. A strong "hero" icon for marketing contexts, less practical for Watch complications.
