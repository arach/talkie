# t-decoration studies — Round 2

14 treatments on the locked lowercase `t`. Extends the A–F catalog from narrative-studio/marks.

## Quiet (G–M)

- **G · stem dot** — Cassette Orange dot at the stem's midpoint
- **H · crossbar slot** — Canvas rectangle cut from the crossbar
- **I · stem foot** — Horizontal tick at the descender end
- **J · cross hairline** — Diagonal hairline crossing the T-junction
- **K · ascender notch** — Canvas square cut from the top of the stem
- **L · crossbar pin** — Cassette Orange dot at the right crossbar terminal
- **M · descender hook accent** — Cassette Orange chip on the hook's inside curve

## Loud (N–T)

- **N · gradient fill** — Cassette Orange → deep amber on the t
- **O · glow halo** — Neon outline glow, ON AIR lamp feel
- **P · debossed** — Stamped into kraft paper, warm shadow
- **Q · chrome** — Brushed silver gradient on the letterform
- **R · tape spool** — Faint cassette reels behind the t
- **S · letterpress** — Ink bleed at the edges, imprecise warmth
- **T · lamp** — Lit indicator dot at the stem's top with halo

## Geometry

Calibrated for JetBrains Mono Medium. stem center ≈ size × 0.276, T-cross ≈ size × 0.469, stem width ≈ size × 0.08.
