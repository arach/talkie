# debossed-kraft

Landmark T stamped into a warm kraft/paper surface. Inner shadow creates the impression of depth — the mark is pressed into the material, not painted on. Hot Mic dot embedded alongside.

**What it evokes.** Tactile, analog, hand-made. Like a letterpress print or a leather-stamped journal cover. The kraft background and grain filter add warmth and texture that feels personal — this is a tool someone owns, not a commodity app.

**Where it shines / where it fails.** The deboss effect is convincing at 512+ and particularly strong on macOS where the paper surface differentiates from the Dock background. Below 64px the depth cues (shadow, highlight) collapse and it reads as a low-contrast flat mark on tan. Needs a darker T fill or stronger shadow for small-size variants. Best for macOS Dock and social cards.
