Talkie_OS

Voice capture and transcription suite for macOS. Fast, private, and powered by on-device AI.

Components
◆ Talkie Engine: Shared background service powering fast local transcription. Private and offline. Used by Talkie and Talkie Live.
■ Talkie: Full library with iCloud sync, workflows, and AI-powered actions.
● Talkie Live: Menu bar app for quick voice capture. Press Option+Command+L to record.
