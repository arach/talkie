Getting Started
1 Open Talkie: Find it in your Applications folder and launch it.
2 Grant Permissions: Allow microphone access when prompted.
3 Start Recording: Press R to record, or use Talkie Live's menu bar icon.
Automatic Setup Helper services (Talkie Live and Talkie Engine) will start automatically at login. You can manage them in Talkie's Settings under Helper Apps.
